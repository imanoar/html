Process

  • Project planning with client

    A profound design process eventually makes the patron, the architect, and every occasional visitor in the building a slightly better human being.

  • Working on draft plan & ideas

    People who build their own home tend to be very courageous. These people are curious about life. They're thinking about what it means to live in a house, rather than just buying a commodity and making it work.

  • Transform into digital assets

    Architecture is really about well-being. I think that people want to feel good in a space … On the one hand it's about shelter, but it's also about pleasure.
